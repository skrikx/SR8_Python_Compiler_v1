# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, professional, and respectful environment for everyone participating in SR8.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, ask clarifying questions, and focus on technical outcomes.
- Provide feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Respect different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or hostile communication.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption of project collaboration.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including:

- issues and pull requests
- discussions and reviews
- any official project communication channels

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately through maintainer channels listed in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
Security-related reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
